Review Process

Understanding the approval workflow

1. Submission

Survey creator submits survey for review with all required information and questions configured.

2. Initial Review

Assigned reviewer examines survey content, question quality, and overall structure.

3. Final Approval

Senior approver gives final approval or requests changes before publication.

4. Publication

Approved survey is published and made available to target audience.

Review Guidelines

What to look for during review

Content Quality

  • Questions are clear and unambiguous
  • No leading or biased questions
  • Appropriate question types for data collection
  • Logical question flow and order

Technical Aspects

  • All questions are properly configured
  • Branching logic works correctly
  • Required fields are marked appropriately
  • Survey settings are correct

Compliance

  • Follows data privacy regulations
  • Includes necessary consent statements
  • Appropriate for target audience
  • Meets company standards
